Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built for people with large local video collections who are tired of endlessly scrolling to decide what to watch.

True Randomisation
One click picks a video at random from your unwatched queue and opens it instantly. No repetition until you've seen everything.
Favourites That Stay
Star a video and it never leaves the queue — even after being watched it comes back for repeat plays. Great for all-time favourites.
Full Library + Queue
Two separate lists: your complete library never loses a file, while the queue shrinks as you watch. Rebuild the queue anytime to start fresh.
Bulk Folder Scanning
Add an entire drive or folder recursively in seconds. Thousands of videos scanned, deduplicated, and added with a single click.
Multi-Select Actions
Shift-click or Ctrl-click to select dozens of videos at once. Mark, favourite, or remove them all in a single action.
Sortable Columns
Click any column header to sort by name, path, last watched, or status. One more click reverses. Third click clears. Simple.
Watched History
Every video you watch is timestamped. See exactly when you last watched anything, filter by watched status, or reset individual entries.
Export & Import
Back up your entire library as a JSON file. Restore it on any PC or merge it with another library. Your data stays yours.
Portable Single EXE
No installer. No Python. No dependencies. One file you copy anywhere — USB drive, shared folder, desktop. Just double-click and go.
